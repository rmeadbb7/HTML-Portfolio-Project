The Best Movies According to Rebecca

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Disneys The Sword in the Stone

This is my favourite cartoon kids movie. I love the songs and the movie reminds me of relaxing sholdooh weekends. It's nostalgia for me.

Black Sheep

Not for the faint hearted, this black humor, graphic movie made me cringe and laugh at the same time.

Agent Argyle

I watched this at the movies this year (2023) and loved the action, plot twists and fun of the whole thing.
